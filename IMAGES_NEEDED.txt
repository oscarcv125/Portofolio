TIPS FOR GOOD SCREENSHOTS
================================================================================

1. Use consistent lighting and color schemes across project screenshots
2. Crop images to focus on the most important UI elements
3. Ensure text in screenshots is readable
4. Use the same device/simulator for iOS app screenshots
5. Consider using mockup generators for a more polished look
6. Add subtle shadows or borders to make images pop
7. Remove any sensitive data or API keys before taking screenshots
